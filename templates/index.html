Who am I?

Hi there! I am a blossoming developer currently wielding Python as my primary tool to bring ideas to life. I found a passion for learning and using new technologies, which is what lead me down the path to become a developer.

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration with a minor in Mathematics, and try to always apply the lessons I've learned to a customer/user-first approach to creation.

Python is my most comfortable language, but I am working to expand my range. I have experience in various libraries and frameworks including but not limited to: Flask, Requests, Selenium, SciKit, NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib, Tkinter among others with even more to learn!

I also have an interest in web; I used Flask, HTML, CSS and Bootstrap to bring this site together. Other tech I'm familiar with includes SQL, JSON, Git, and I'm always trying to learn more.

My goals as a developer and as a human are to learn new skills every day or build on the one's that I already have. If a role or a project requires me to learn new technologies or even entirely new languages I am excited for the opportunity.

My approach to development, education and life is to try not to reinvent the wheel when I could be just making a better wheel.

Although computing may be binary, I am not (she/they pronouns please and thank you)! You can call me Lily, L.R. or just L, I am more than happy with whichever you prefer.

Much of my free-time lately has been devoted to learning but when I'm not tackling a new skill I mostly enjoy gaming. Tabletop games like Magic the Gathering and MMOs like OSRS, WoW and FFXIV are among my favorites.
